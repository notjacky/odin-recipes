Crockpot Chicken Recipe

Description

Servings Size: 4, Prep Time: 5 minutes, Cooking Time: 8 hours

Ingredients

  • 16 bottle Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmasan cheese
  • 4 chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Stir dressing and cheese in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, pour dressing to cover chicken.
  3. Cook for 8 hours
