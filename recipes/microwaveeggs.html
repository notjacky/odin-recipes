Microwave Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Serving Size: 1, Prep Time: 2 minutes, Cook Time: 2.5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Break eggs in a bowl, add salt and milk. Mix.
  2. Place bowl in microwave and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Mix and scrap sides.
  4. Repeat steps 2 to 4 every 30 seconds for 2.5 minutes.
