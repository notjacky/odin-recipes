Potato Soup Recipe

Description

Serving Size: 6, Prep Time: 5 minutes, Cooking Time: 3.5 hours

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound bacon
  • 28 ounce bag of potatoes
  • 30 ounces of chicken broth
  • 8 ounces garlic and cheese spread
  • 1 pinch black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook bacon on medium heat on skillet until browned, soak and chop them up.
  2. Cook potatos until browned on the same skillet.
  3. Add potatoes to slow cooker, then chicken broth, cheese spread and bacon.
  4. Cook until potatoes are tender, estimated 3-4 hours.
